ShopEase

Site Name

ShopEase - This name represents a modern, easy-to-use online store for curated products. (Optional domain: shopease.com)

Site Purpose

The purpose of ShopEase is to provide a seamless and enjoyable online shopping experience. The site will offer a curated selection of products, detailed product pages, a dynamic shopping cart, and secure checkout with Stripe. An admin panel will allow for inventory and order management.

Scenarios

  • How can I find and purchase a specific product?
  • What payment methods are supported and is my information secure?
  • How do I contact customer support or track my order?

Color Schema

  • #008751 (Green): Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • #333333 (Dark Gray): Used for body text and footer background.
  • #f5f5f5 (Light Gray): Used for page backgrounds.

Typography

  • Playfair Display - Used for main headings and logo.
  • Roboto - Used for body text and navigation.
